About Rugby

Rugby is a popular team sport originating in England in the 19th century. It is played by two teams of fifteen players each, using an oval-shaped ball on a rectangular field. The objective of the game is to score points by carrying, passing, kicking, or grounding the ball in the opposing team's end zone.

Teams
