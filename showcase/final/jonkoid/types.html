Types of Games

Action games

Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed is a popular action-adventure game series known for its historical settings and stealth-based gameplay.

Role-playing games (RPGs)

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt.

In this game, players take on the role of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, as they navigate a richly detailed open world, make choices that impact the story, and engage in combat and exploration.

Adventure games

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is known for its open-world gameplay, stunning graphics, and immersive storytelling.

Puzzle games

Tetris

In this game, players must strategically place falling blocks to create complete lines and prevent the screen from filling up. It's a classic and challenging puzzle game that has been enjoyed by players for decades.

Sports games

FIFA

FIFA is the best soccer simulation game that allows players to control their favorite teams and compete in various leagues and tournaments.
