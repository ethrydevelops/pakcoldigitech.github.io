Chappell Roan

About

Kayleigh Rose Amstutz, known professionally as Chappell Roan, is an American singer and songwriter, known for her bold and genre-blending music, which mixes elements of pop, alternative, and indie. Emerging in the music scene with a distinctive voice and powerful presence, Roan has gained attention for her emotive lyrics and unique sound. Chappell Roan is celebrated for her unapologetic approach to her art and her ability to tackle personal and societal themes through her music.
