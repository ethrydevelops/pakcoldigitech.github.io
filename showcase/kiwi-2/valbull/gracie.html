Gracie Abrams

About

Gracie Abrams is an American singer-songwriter known for her introspective and emotionally resonant music. She gained attention with her debut single "Mean It" in 2019 and has continued to build her career with a series of well-received singles and her debut EP, Minor. Abrams' music often features a blend of pop and indie influences, with a focus on personal and relatable themes. Her delicate vocals and poignant lyrics have earned her praise from critics and fans alike, establishing her as a promising and distinctive voice in contemporary music.

Discography

Her discography includes:

  • "Minor" (2020)
  • "This Is What the Truth Feels Like" (2021)
  • "Good Riddance" (2023)
  • "The Secret of Us" (2024)
