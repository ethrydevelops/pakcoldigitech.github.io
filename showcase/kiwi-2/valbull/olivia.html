Olivia Rodrigo

About

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress who gained widespread recognition with her debut single "drivers license" in early 2021. Her debut album, SOUR, was a critical and commercial success, featuring a blend of pop, rock, and alt-pop influences. Known for her raw, emotional lyrics and powerful vocals, Rodrigo's music often explores themes of heartbreak, self-discovery, and youth. Her debut work earned her numerous awards and accolades, establishing her as one of the standout new artists of her generation.
