Sabrina Carpenter

About

Sabrina Carpenter is an American singer, songwriter, and actress who first gained prominence through her role on the Disney Channel show Girl Meets World. She transitioned into music with her debut EP in 2014 and has since released several albums, blending pop and indie influences. Carpenter is known for her catchy melodies and relatable lyrics, often exploring themes of youth, empowerment, and personal growth. She has built a strong fan base with her music and continues to evolve as an artist, receiving praise for her vocal ability and engaging performances.

Discography

Her discography includes:

  • "Can’t Blame a Girl for Trying" (2014)
  • "Eyes Wide Open" (2015)
  • "Evolution" (2016)
  • "Singular: Act I" (2018)
  • "Singular: Act II" (2019)
  • "Emails I Can’t Send" (2022)
  • "Emails I Can’t Send: Fwd" (2023)
